Trevor began his career with the Department of Natural Resources as an emergency firefighter in the Eastern Region in 1979, fighting fires and servicing fire equipment in Lac du Bonnet. In April of 1983 Trevor was hired as a crewmember on the Lac du Bonnet Firetac crew, and was promoted to Crew Leader in 1985. This was a position which he held for many years before joining the Provincial Air Attack Program in 1996. In 2013, Trevor was promoted to the Supervisor of the Air Attack Program, providing leadership and developing new staff over the years. In his new role as Fire Operations Manager, Trevor has been responsible for overseeing the Provincial Air Attack contract and providing leadership on all aspects of Aviation Management.
